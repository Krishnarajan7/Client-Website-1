Vedha Clothing: Fashion Meets Tradition

Inaugurated on May 19, 2024, by Janana Sankri, Vedha Clothing has quickly made its mark as a go-to destination for those seeking both traditional and contemporary attire.


Since its inception, Vedha Clothing has been committed to providing high-quality readymade garments that cater to the diverse tastes and needs of our customers. Situated at the heart of Thiruvarur, we take pride in offering not just fashion but a lifestyle that blends elegance, comfort, and tradition.


Under the leadership of Janana Sankri, Vedha Clothing is not only growing in popularity but also in vision. The shop, which operates under the esteemed Amman Groups, has plans to expand its horizons by introducing an exclusive women’s clothing line. With this new addition, we aim to bring our unique touch to women's fashion by creating our own styles and designs, ensuring that every woman feels confident, stylish, and empowered.


Vedha Clothing is more than just a retail outlet; it’s a brand that’s gradually becoming synonymous with quality, authenticity, and innovation. As we continue to grow, our commitment to bringing the best in fashion will remain at the core of what we do. Whether you're looking for everyday wear or something special, Vedha Clothing is here to serve all your fashion needs.


Stay tuned for more updates as we embark on this exciting journey toward creating a line of clothing that celebrates every woman’s individuality and style.
